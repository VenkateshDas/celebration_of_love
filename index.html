Our Story

In the days of our college, we met as strangers. We developed an acquaintance, as is common in the fleeting moments of youth. But fate had other plans for us.

Time passed, and we found ourselves crossing paths once more. This time, our friendship blossomed and grew deeper with each passing day. Though we both sensed there was something more to our connection, we never ventured down that path. We were content to simply be the best of friends.

Distance and time separated us, and though we were physically apart, our bond only grew stronger. We confided in each other, laughed and cried together, and always knew we had a friend who truly understood us.

But as the years went by, we both knew that our friendship had grown into something more. Finally, after four years of knowing each other, we decided to make it official and commit to each other. It was not an easy decision, but it was one that we knew deep in our hearts was right.

The road ahead was not without its challenges, as we had to convince our families to understand and accept our love. But through it all, we remained united and committed to each other.

And now, after 6 years we are proud to say that we have triumphed over all personal and cultural obstacles and are happily in love. Our journey has been a winding one, but one that has led us to where we are today. We are grateful for the memories we've made and the love we've found, and we look forward to many more adventures to come.
